Whether you're going on vacation, have a special occasion, or simply want to switch things up, our platform adapts to your needs. We are here to support your journey to better health, offering you the tools and flexibility to make nutritious eating a lasting part of your lifestyle.

Stress-Free Delivery

We ensure stress-free delivery of your customized meal plan at your doorstep to ensure that you stay focused on your fitness goals.

How Are My Meals Delivered?

We understand the importance of freshness when it comes to your meals. That's why we've perfected the art of meal delivery to ensure that every bite you take is as fresh and delicious as the moment it was prepared. When you choose our service, your meals are meticulously packaged and delivered with care. They arrive at your doorstep in an insulated cooler bag, complete with ice packs that maintain a chilly temperature for an impressive 6-8 hours from the time of delivery drop. This means your meals remain perfectly cold and safe for consumption even if you're not at home when they arrive. To preserve the quality and nutritional value of your meals, we recommend consuming them within 24 hours of delivery and storing them chilled, ideally below 5 degrees Celsius. Our delivery vans are equipped with refrigeration units to ensure that your meals stay fresh during transit. This dual-layered approach, combining insulated packaging and refrigerated delivery, guarantees that your meals are not only convenient but also of the highest quality, promoting your health and well-being with every delicious bite.

Where and when do you deliver?

Aleen’s strives to make our meal delivery service as convenient and accessible as possible. We deliver daily, except on Sundays, ensuring you have access to freshly prepared, health-conscious meals throughout the week. Our delivery slots are designed to accommodate a variety of schedules, and they depend on your specific location. Typically, deliveries are made between 2 a.m. and 8 a.m., giving you the flexibility to receive your meals when it's most convenient for you. We also understand that you may have specific time requests, and we do our best to accommodate them based on availability. To keep you informed and updated, we send you a notification once your delivery is completed, providing you with peace of mind. In our commitment to sustainability, we’ve implem ented biodegradable packaging. After enjoying your meals, simply keep the empty cooler bag with the ice packs at your doorstep for courier collection on the second day of the delivery drop. While we currently serve Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, and Ajman, we're continually expanding our reach. If we're not yet delivering to your area, rest assured that we're working hard to extend our service to new locations.

Do I have to be at home to accept my delivery?

No, you don't need to be at home to accept delivery of your carefully curated meal box. We understand that your schedule may not always align with our delivery slots, and that's perfectly fine. To ensure a smooth delivery experience, we recommend communicating with your building's security team to grant access to our courier. Your health and convenience are our top priorities, which is why we've designed our service to be as accommodating as possible. If you have any specific delivery instructions or preferences, you can easily communicate them by placing notes in the designated delivery tab or by reaching out to our dedicated customer care team. Rest assured that your delicious, health-conscious meals will be waiting safely your doorstep, packed with care and precision. We've taken every step to make sure you can enjoy your meals at your convenience, whether you're at home or on the go. Your well-being is our mission, and we're here to make healthy eating effortless for you.
